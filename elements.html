About Our Product

Our plans for Odyssee Vr

Odyssee Vr is a interactive and educational game designed to provide a solution to the education problem in America and the world The game currently has three subjects: Science, Math, Music. These subjects will have sub categories. For example Science has Chemistry, Earth Science, and Biology. In the future we plan to have different educational levels such as Middle school, College, etc...
